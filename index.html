Ilm For Success Youth (i4SY)

 

"Deep rooted trees grow strong and give shade"

The generation with great potential, high intelligence, capability, and vision is too often misunderstood and unfairly labelled as lost or misguided. The mission of i4SY is to reshape this narrative by fostering a generation firmly grounded in Islamic values. Through a gradual and structured approach, we aim to cultivate individuals who grow with strength and purpose, ultimately becoming a source of guidance and support for those who come after them.

Our Services

 

Courses

................

Unlock a deeper understanding of our religion with our comprehensive, custom-designed courses. Tailored for different age groups, these programs empower youth to reflect on key lessons and apply them in their daily lives, fostering a lifelong love for seeking ilm.

Guided by dedicated and passionate instructors, each session offers personalized support and engages students through interactive activities, quizzes, and healthy competitions, with real-time rewards to motivate and inspire.  

 

Community

................

Join vibrant, age and gender-segregated community spaces designed to strengthen the bonds of sisterhood and brotherhood in Islam. These supportive environments offer tailored resources, challenges, events, discussions, and guidance relevant to their life.

Thrive both academically and socially through our dedicated platforms and programs, where faith and community grow together. 

 

Coaching

................

Our Coaching Service is a dynamic initiative designed to nurture the minds and hearts of young individuals, equipping them with the tools they need to grow in confidence, character, and purpose. Through a faith-based, values-driven approach, we guide youth on a journey of self-discovery, spiritual development, and personal excellence.

Led by certified and experienced coaches, this program offers a safe and inspiring space for young people to explore their identity, overcome challenges, set meaningful goals, and build essential life skills.
